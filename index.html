About Me

I'm William

Full Stack Developer

Welcome to my corner of the digital world! I'm William Scanlin, a Full-Stack developer based in the vibrant city of Chicago, Illinois. My journey into the world of technology began with a foundation in biology—a Bachelor's degree from Purdue University—that initially led me into the captivating realm of microbiology.

In my role as a laboratory director and microbiologist, I immersed myself in the intricacies of laboratory management, quality control, and regulatory compliance within the food testing industry. Over the course of seven years, I honed my skills, contributing to projects such as implementing ISO 17025 accreditation policies and managing comparative analyses for USDA-FSIS studies.

However, my fascination with technology and its potential for innovation prompted me to expand my horizons. I embarked on a journey to acquire skills in Full-Stack development, blending my scientific expertise with the artistry of web development. Leveraging my analytical chemistry background and hands-on experience with instruments like High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Gas Chromatography (GC), I found parallels in the meticulous nature of coding.

Driven by a passion for continuous learning, I delved into Cyber Technology, exploring network security tools such as Kali Linux and Wireshark. This additional expertise not only broadened my skill set but also opened doors to new opportunities in the realm of Laboratory Information Management Systems (LIMS).

Today, I stand at the intersection of science and technology—a laboratory LIMS professional with Full-Stack capabilities. I have experience networking full laboratories for the cannabis industry, integrating real-time Polymerase Chain Reaction (rt-PCR) systems, and building Linux-based SQL servers for data management.

Furthermore, I've facilitated cloud LIMS integration and implemented track and trace capabilities to enhance laboratory sample management. With a holistic approach to problem-solving and a keen eye for detail, I strive to revolutionize laboratory operations, streamlining processes for optimal efficiency and accuracy.

Whether you're looking to elevate your laboratory's capabilities, develop cutting-edge applications, or simply navigate the ever-evolving digital landscape, I'm here to bring your vision to life. Join me as we embark on a journey of innovation, creativity, and endless possibilities.

Welcome to the world of William Scanlin, where science meets technology in perfect harmony.
